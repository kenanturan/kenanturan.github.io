Taktik Mücadelenin Temelleri

(Fundamentals of Tactical Combat: From Field Experience to Academic Theory)

Authors: Tugay Barış Güneş, Kenan Turan Publisher: Nobel Akademik Yayıncılık Year: March, 2021 ISBN: 978-625-439-130-9

Abstract:

While combat systems and programs host universal features; crime types, attack varieties, individual armament levels, and cultural factors vary by country. Therefore, the reference points of combat methods need to be determined, adapted to changing conditions, and be renewable. This book is prepared to reveal the fundamental components of the combat process and create awareness about the necessity of handling them with both an integrated and dynamic understanding.

Academic Thesis

The Role of Turkish Resistance Organization (TMT) in the Establishment of TRNC

Master's Thesis

Author: Kenan Turan Advisor: Dr. Öğr. Üyesi Gökhan Ak University: Istanbul Topkapi University Institute: Institute of Graduate Studies, Dept. of Security Sciences Year: 2024 Thesis No: 893753

Abstract:

This study deeply examines the strategic and historical role played by the Turkish Resistance Organization (TMT) in the establishment of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus (TRNC). The research addresses the activities conducted by TMT to protect and develop the Turkish presence in Cyprus, and the social, cultural, and political effects of these activities on the Turkish Cypriot Community. The resistance conducted by TMT against EOKA's ENOSIS movement and its effects during the TRNC establishment process are analyzed within international relations context.
